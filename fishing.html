Fishing in northern Jämtland!

In Jämtland there are a large number of fishing waters

Fly fishing in the streams

Welcome to explore more than 200km of flowing noble fishing waters. Explore the mythical Rotälven, home to elusive trout, or venture into the secluded Tennån and Vanån rivers. It is not for nothing that fly fishing become something of a Jämtland landmark.

Fishing in lakes & ponds

Here with us you will find 100s of different trout and char lakes with unlimited opportunities for experiences. Boat fishing, trolling, baitcasting, reeling, fly or fishing from a floating ring. It is just to choose.

Fishing regulations

All flowing waters except Österdalälven and part of Rotälven, fishing ban 1/9 until 30/4.

In Österdalälven fishing ban 1/9 until 15/5. However fishing for grayling with fly is allowed 1/9 and in parts of Rotälven, from the confluence with Österdalälven up to Jöllenbron, fishing for grayling with fly is allowed 1/9 to until 31/12.

General fishing ban in all fall planted waters 1/9 - 30/11.
